About Us

Welcome to FitBeau.com. Our influencer marketing platform where brands can match with content creators to promote their fitness and beauty products

FitBeau was inspired by a fitness and beauty directory site the founder built in 2008 which used facebook and twitter to allow businesses to use their own customers to do their ‘advertising’ for them. At the time Facebook was 2 years old and twitter only had Steven Fry as their celeb advocate but this was the very start of the future of social media for businesses. With our deep understanding of the evolution of social media, we want to share our insights so you can capitalise on the newest trends fast, so you stay ahead of the curve.mple, we keep you in control Help us shape the app to meet your needs and relieve you of some of those pains.
